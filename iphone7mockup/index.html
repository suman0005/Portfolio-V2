Food Delivery App

A modern mobile application designed to connect users with local restaurants for seamless food ordering and delivery

Project Overview

This food delivery app is designed to provide users with a delightful experience when ordering meals from local restaurants. The interface prioritizes beautiful food photography, intuitive navigation, and a streamlined ordering process.

  • Personalized restaurant recommendations based on user preferences
  • Seamless payment integration with multiple options
  • Detailed restaurant profiles with ratings and reviews
  • Easy reordering of favorite meals with one tap
Back to Projects

Design Process

The design process began with extensive research into user behavior when ordering food online. I conducted interviews with frequent food delivery app users to understand their preferences, pain points, and expectations.

After creating initial wireframes, I focused on developing a visual language that emphasizes food photography and creates an appetizing experience. The color palette features warm, inviting tones that stimulate appetite, while the interface elements are designed to be intuitive and accessible even when users are hungry and in a hurry.
