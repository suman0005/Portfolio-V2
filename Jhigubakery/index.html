Jhigu Bakery App

A delightful mobile application for ordering fresh baked goods with seamless user experience

Project Overview

The Jhigu Bakery App is designed to provide customers with a convenient way to browse, order, and customize bakery products. The app features a clean, appetizing interface that showcases the bakery's products in an enticing way.

  • Intuitive product browsing with high-quality images
  • Customization options for cakes and pastries
  • Seamless checkout and payment process
Back to Projects

Design Process

The design process for the Jhigu Bakery App began with extensive research into user behavior when ordering food online. I conducted interviews with bakery customers to understand their preferences and pain points with existing food ordering apps.

After creating initial wireframes, I focused on developing a visual language that evokes warmth and freshness—qualities associated with freshly baked goods. The color palette features warm tones complemented by clean whites to create an appetizing yet modern aesthetic.
