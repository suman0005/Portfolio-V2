Coca-Cola App Design

Coca-Cola, as a global beverage brand, has a strong identity — but there's room to imagine how its digital/mobile presence could feel more immersive and interactive. This concept reimagines how users could explore and buy Coca-Cola products with ease and excitement.

Project Overview

To design a modern, user-friendly mobile interface for Coca-Cola that enhances product discovery, brand experience, and in-app purchase convenience.

  • Bold Landing Page with strong branding, visual appeal, and call-to-action
  • Product Detail Page with nutritional info, rating, and price
  • Cart Functionality with quantity control and seamless purchase option
  • User-Friendly Navigation with bottom tab bar (Home, Favorites, Cart, Profile)
